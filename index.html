FAQ

Common questions before submitting.

What file formats do you work with?

We accept all major CAD and production formats — STEP, DXF, DWG, STL, PDF markups, and native files from SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Fusion 360, Inventor, CATIA, and Creo. If your format is not listed, note it in the requirement summary and we will confirm compatibility before quoting.

How is pricing calculated?

All work is quoted per project after scope review. There are no day rates, retainers, or upfront charges. You receive a fixed cost and delivery timeline before approving. Work only starts after your confirmation — no exceptions.

What happens if the output does not match the requirement?

Deliverables are checked against the agreed brief before release. Defined revisions within the original scope are included. If the requirement changes after approval, revised scope and cost are agreed before any additional work is scheduled.

Do I need to sign an NDA before sharing files?

No technical files are needed at initial intake — the first form captures the requirement summary only. NDA review is available before any drawings or models are shared. Request this at any point before or after initial submission.

Is there a minimum project size or volume commitment?

No minimum volume or ongoing commitment is required. MIDTS supports individual overflow tasks as well as repeat production requirements. Each submission is reviewed and quoted independently.

How long does the initial review take?

Initial review is typically completed within one UK business day. If clarification is needed before quoting, we will contact you by the work email provided on the intake form.
